Bulalo Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. beef shanks with bone marrow
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2-3 pieces of corn on the cob, cut into thirds
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 tbsp calamansi juice (or lime juice)
  • 1 small cabbage, quartered
  • 2-3 tablespoons of fish sauce (patis)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water
  • Optional: green onions and chili peppers for garnish

    • Instructions

  • In a large pot, bring enough water to cover the beef shanks to a boil.
    Blanch the beef shanks for about 5-10 minutes to remove impurities. Drain and discard the water.
  • Add fresh water to the pot, enough to cover the beef shanks again. Bring to a boil and then reduce the heat to a simmer.
  • Add the chopped onions and minced garlic to the pot, and continue to simmer for about 2-3 hours or until the beef shanks are tender.
  • Add the cubed potatoes and corn on the cob to the pot, and continue to simmer for another 20-30 minutes or until the vegetables are cooked.
  • Add the quartered cabbage to the pot and continue to simmer for another 5-10 minutes or until the cabbage is cooked but still slightly crunchy.
  • Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste.
  • Serve hot in bowls with the beef shanks, vegetables, and broth. Garnish with green onions and chili peppers, if desired.
