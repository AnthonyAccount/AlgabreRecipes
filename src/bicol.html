Bicol Express Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. pork belly, sliced into small pieces
  • 2 cups of coconut milk
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-2 chili peppers (e.g. Thai chili or bird's eye chili), chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil for frying

    • Instructions

  • Heat a skillet or a wok with cooking oil over medium-high heat.
  • Add the sliced pork belly and sauté until it is slightly browned.
  • Add the diced onions and minced garlic, and sauté until the onions are translucent.
  • Add the chopped chili peppers and sauté for a few minutes.
  • Add the shrimp paste and stir well.
  • Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a boil.
  • Lower the heat and let the Bicol Express simmer for about 20-30 minutes or until the pork is tender and the sauce has thickened.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Serve hot with steamed rice.
